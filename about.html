Meet Grandma Lillian

...

Lillian was born in 1904 and lived to 104 years. She learned to cook from her mother and grandmother, survived two world wars, and raised a family through frugality, home-grown food, and traditional values.

Her legacy inspires this site—helping modern families rediscover the timeless habits that truly nourish us.

“My Nan, Lillian, lived to 104, eating home-cooked meals every day. This site is my way of sharing her wisdom.” — Gary
